MYSURU

Mysore is one of the most prominent tourist areas of India. Mysore is also known as the Palace City of India.

COORG / MADIKERI

Coorg is known as Scotland of India. It is a Hill Station. It is one of the top destinations of Karnataka tourism. Coorg is the largest producer of Coffe in India. It is one of the places with highest rainfall in India. Coorg is famous for trekking activities.

COASTAL REGION/ KARAVALI

This is coastal strip of Karnataka lies. The 3 district are Uttara Kannada and Dakshina Kannada and Udupi district.

BENGALURU

It is the capital city of Karnataka State.It is known as the "Garden City of India" because of its greenery and many public parks.It is also called as the Silicon Valley of India because of the large number of information technology companies located in the city.

HAMPI / HAMPE

Hampi or Hampe (in Kannada), also referred to as the Group of Monuments at Hampi, is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.It was the capital of Vijayanagara Empire in the 14th century.

HUBLI - DHARWAD

This are the two twin Cities of Karnataka.Hubli is famous for its handloom textiles and for being a trading hub of iron and cotton. Dharwad acted as a gateway between the Malenadu region and the plains, and it became a resting place for travelers.
